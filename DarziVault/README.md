## 🎯 The Problem We Saw

Local tailors in Pakistan still rely on old paper notebooks or loose notes for client measurements. The challenges are real:

- 📝 **Lost or Damaged Notes** — Important client measurements get lost, torn, or unreadable over time.
- 🔍 **Hard to Search** — Finding a client’s old measurements takes too much time.
- ✏️ **Difficult to Update** — When a client gains/loses weight, editing old records is messy.
- 📞 **Repeat Orders Slow** — Tailors waste time asking for measurements again for old clients.
- 🧠 **No Organization** — No proper history, no garment-wise records, and no professional feel.

> *Result?* Wasted time, delayed deliveries, frustrated clients, and missed business opportunities.

---

## 💡 The Solution We Built

**DarziVault** is a digital measurement vault made for real tailors. Simple, fast, and completely offline — just like their old notebook, but smarter and more powerful.

| Problem                    | Our Solution |
|---------------------------|--------------|
| Lost Paper Notes          | Secure digital storage with Hive |
| Hard to Find Records      | Fast search + client history |
| Editing Measurements      | Easy edit with version history |
| Slow Repeat Orders        | One-tap access to previous measurements |
| Unprofessional Look       | Clean, premium app experience |

### 🧠 What Makes DarziVault Different?
- **100% Offline** — Works perfectly without internet
- **Urdu + English** — Full bilingual support
- **Tailor-First Design** — Large buttons, simple navigation, made for real darzis
- **Multiple Measurement Sets** — Keep history of different dates/garments
- **Voice Input** — Speak measurements instead of typing
- **PDF Export** — Share professional reports via WhatsApp

## 📘 Scope of the DarziVault App

**DarziVault** is a fully offline tailor measurement management app that helps local tailors save client details and body measurements digitally in a safe, searchable, and editable format.

---

## 🎮 Core Modules

### 1. Dashboard
- Quick stats (Total Clients, Measurements, Orders)
- Recent clients
- Quick action buttons

### 2. Clients Management
- Add, view, search, and edit clients
- Photo + contact + notes support

### 3. Measurements Vault (Core Feature)
- Multiple dated measurement records per client
- Categorized tabs: General, Upper Body, Lower Body, Garment Specific
- Easy + / - buttons + Voice Input
- Copy from previous measurements

### 4. Orders Management
- Create stitching orders linked with clients
- Track delivery dates and status

### 5. Reports & Export
- Beautiful PDF measurement reports
- Share via WhatsApp

---

## 🛠 Core Features

- **Fully Offline** using Hive database
- **Bilingual** (English + Urdu with RTL support)
- **Voice Input** for fast measurement entry
- **PDF Generation** & sharing
- **Auto-save** while entering measurements
- **Large, Clear UI** suitable for tailors
- **Data Backup & Restore** (Export/Import)
- **Dark & Light Theme**

### ✅ Technical Highlights
- Built with **Flutter** & **Riverpod**
- Fast local database with **Hive**
- Clean MVVM architecture
- No internet required
- Optimized for low-end Android devices

### 🚫 Out of Scope (Current Version)
- Online cloud sync
- Multi-user / shop staff login
- Payment integration
- Advanced analytics dashboard
